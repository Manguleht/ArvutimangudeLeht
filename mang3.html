What is Doom

Doom is a legendary first-person shooter (FPS) game that revolutionized the gaming industry. Released in 1993 by id Software, Doom is known for its pioneering graphics, intense gameplay, and iconic multiplayer experience. It has set the standard for the FPS genre and remains a landmark title in the history of video games.

How to play it

Objective

The objective of Doom is to navigate through various levels, battling demons and other monsters. Players must find keys, solve puzzles, and survive hordes of enemies to progress through the game.

Review

Doom, the original first-person shooter, is a timeless masterpiece that laid the foundation for modern gaming. Its fast-paced action, atmospheric design, and groundbreaking multiplayer mode have left an indelible mark on the gaming community. Whether you're a nostalgic fan or a newcomer, Doom offers an adrenaline-pumping experience that continues to captivate players.
