Solitaire Review

Objective:

The objective of Solitaire is to move all cards to a foundation, organized by suit and in ascending order. The game is won when all cards are moved to the foundation.

Gameplay:

Grid:

The game is played on a grid, traditionally a square grid, and the size of the grid can vary.

Mines:

Numbers:

When a player clicks on a cell, it reveals either an empty space, a number, or a mine. If a number is revealed, it indicates the number of mines in the adjacent cells.

Flags:

Players can mark cells they suspect contain mines by placing flags on them. This is crucial for strategic gameplay.

Winning:

The game is won when all non-mine cells are revealed. Players must use logic and deduction to uncover safe cells and avoid mines.

Score: 8/10
